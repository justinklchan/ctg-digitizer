CTG Digitizer

Drop cardiotocography strips (PDF or image) — each is digitized automatically into a CSV time series. Traces are separated by panel; the axis scale is read off the strip.
🔒 Runs entirely in your browser. Your strips are never uploaded — only this page's code is downloaded, the images stay on your computer.
